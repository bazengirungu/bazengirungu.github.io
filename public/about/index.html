About Me

Hi, I’m Duncan Maina — a cybersecurity trainer, SOC analyst, digital forensics practitioner, and researcher in cyber resilience. I thrive at the intersection of technical security, education, and real-world problem solving, with a passion for building practical, impact-driven security solutions.

I currently work as a Cybersecurity Trainer at Cybershujaa, where I mentor and train students in offensive security, defensive operations, digital forensics, and cyber risk management. I also deliver professional lectures and talks under ACIC at institutions including the Kenya School of Government (KSG), University of Nairobi (UoN), and Serianu.

What I Do
#

  • Security Operations & Blue Teaming

    • SOC monitoring, threat detection, and incident response
    • EDR, SIEM, and SOAR integrations
    • Log analysis and threat hunting

  • Offensive Security

    • Web, network, and infrastructure penetration testing
    • CTF design and lab environment deployment (B2R, exam-style machines)
    • Reconnaissance & OSINT operations

  • Digital Forensics

    • Evidence acquisition & analysis
    • Log, memory, and disk forensics
    • Incident reconstruction and reporting

  • Cyber Risk & Resilience

    • Cybersecurity maturity assessments
    • Risk quantification and KPI dashboards
    • Control frameworks and governance alignment

  • Training & Mentorship

    • Curriculum development
    • Student performance review
    • Exam setting and grading
    • Hands-on lab facilitation
